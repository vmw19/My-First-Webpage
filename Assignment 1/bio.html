Biography

Greetings! I am Victoria and I absolutely love challenges. I am a veteran elementary education teacher with over 12 years of classroom experience. I love learning and I enjoy teaching students, especially students with special needs exceptionalities. My first intrduction to coding was in the classroom. My students with behavior plans would always ask if they could code after comppleting their work! Coding really motivated my students in the classroom! Coding allowed my students with so many challenges and differences to identify their strengths.
